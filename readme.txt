Product FAQs WooCommerce helps store owners manage FAQs on product pages to improve user experience and increase conversions.

== Description ==

Enhance your WooCommerce store with the Product FAQs WooCommerce plugin, designed to help you provide clear and concise answers to common customer inquiries. With this user-friendly plugin, you can easily create and manage FAQs for your products, improving customer experience and boosting conversions.

### Key Features: ###
- ** Seamless Integration: Effortlessly integrates with your existing WooCommerce setup, requiring no additional configurations.**
- ** Easy FAQ Management: Add, edit, or delete FAQs directly from the product edit page in the WordPress admin dashboard.**
- ** Customizable Appearance: Choose from various display styles to match your store's branding, ensuring FAQs look great on any device.**
- ** SEO Friendly: Optimize your FAQs for search engines, improving visibility and driving more traffic to your store.**
- ** User Interaction: Allow customers to submit their own questions, making your FAQs dynamic and engaging.**
- ** Multilingual Support: Fully compatible with WPML, ensuring your FAQs are accessible to customers in multiple languages.**

### Benefits:###
- ** Reduce Customer Inquiries: Address common questions upfront, reducing the number of support requests and freeing up your time.**
- ** Boost Sales: By providing clear information, you can help customers make informed purchasing decisions, leading to higher conversion rates.**
- ** Improve User Experience: Enhance customer satisfaction by making information easily accessible, fostering trust and loyalty.**
- ** Get started today with the Product FAQs WooCommerce plugin and transform the way you communicate with your customers!**

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the screen in WordPress

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP 7.3 or greater is recommended
* MySQL 5.6 or greater is recommended
